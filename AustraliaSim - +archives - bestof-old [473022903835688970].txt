__**CSID Cookies**__
__Ingredients__
115g plain flour
40g self raising flour
3tbsp butter
55g light brown sugar
55g caster sugar
1 egg
1 tsp cinnamon
Choc chips to tase

__Method__
Brown the butter, then set in fridge.
Mix together the 2 sugars, then add the flour and cinnamon until combined.
Mix in egg to the powdered ingredients.
Melt browned butter, then pour in slowly while mixing the egg and powders.
Knead with hands once too stiff for mixing.
Add choc chips and knead in.
Chill dough overnight.
Preheat oven to 160c the next morning.
Warm and knead dough.
Roll out golfball sized lumps of dough, then squash into cookies with hand.
Cook for 10 minutes, then turn off and leave cookies in oven for approx 3-5 minutes.
Let cool and eat.
